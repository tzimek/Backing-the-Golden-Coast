BACKPACKING LEVELS

Read through each level to make sure you are doing a trail that fits your comfort level. There are 3 different levels and they all share average mileage and elevation gains throughout the trip. Try to push yourself, but also make sure to stay safe!

    Beginner

    Expect to backpack about 5-7 miles a day, with 1,000-1,500 feet of elevation. This type of hike could also be considered as a day hike.


    Moderate

    You will be backpacking about 7-12 miles a day, with 1,500-3,000 feet of elevation. Bring lots of water, the elevation gains may be literally breathtaking ;)


    Difficult

    Be prepared to backpack 12-20 miles a day, with 3,000-5,000 feet of elevation. Make sure to leave super early so you have a full day to complete the trail.
